Emma

In a two week university project called Think.Make.Start we developed a system to tackle the problem of bad presentations. We started by finding out what people are most annoyed by during presentations.

We developed EMMA - a system consisting of a smart speaker device and an app that allows to get feedback for presentations.

Get feedback for monotony, pace, volume ...

Find more information about the project on our website and take a look at our journey
